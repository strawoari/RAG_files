長者電費援助計劃
澳電致力履行企業社會責任，提供合資格的長者客戶每月首88度電的11%折扣優惠。
申請條件：
1 持有頤老咭的澳門永久居民；
2 所登記的安裝地址*必須為住宅用途；
3 收費類別為A1或A2的客戶。
*每張頤老咭只能登記一個安裝地址
所需文件：
1 澳門永久居民身份證；
2 頤老咭；
3 住址證明或聲明書；及
4 該住址的最新一期電費單。
查詢詳情，歡迎致電資詢通 2833 9911 。

Senior Citizen Support Program
CEM strives to demonstrate our commitment to the social interest of the community. Eligible senior customers can enjoy an 11% discount on the first 88 kWh of electricity consumption.
Application requirements
1 Permanent Macau resident and holder of Senior Citizen Card; 
2 The registered installation address* must be for residential use;
3 Tariff A1 or A2 customer
*Only one installation address can be registered per Senior Citizen Card
Required documents
1 Macau Permanent Resident Identity Card;
2 Senior Citizen Card;
3 Evidence or declaration of household address; and
4 Latest electricity bill of declared household address.
